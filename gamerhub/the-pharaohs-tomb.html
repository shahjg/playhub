HOW TO PLAY
OBJECTIVE
Explore an ancient Egyptian tomb in 3D. Solve 5 puzzles across 3 chambers to escape before time runs out.

  2. Point at glowing objects — the crosshair turns gold
  3. Click EXAMINE to interact with objects and puzzles
  4. Use navigation arrows to move between chambers
  5. Select inventory items to use them on puzzles
