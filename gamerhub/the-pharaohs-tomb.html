EXIT The Pharaoh's Tomb
HOW TO PLAY
OBJECTIVE
You're trapped in an ancient Egyptian tomb. Solve 5 puzzles across 3 chambers to find the exit before time runs out.
GAMEPLAY
  1. Click glowing markers in each chamber to discover puzzles
  2. Solve puzzles to collect artifacts and unlock new chambers
  3. Items in your inventory can be used on certain puzzles
  4. Navigate between chambers using the arrow buttons
  5. Solve all puzzles and reach the exit before 25 minutes
WITH FRIENDS
