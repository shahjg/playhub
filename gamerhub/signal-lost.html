EXIT Signal Lost
HOW TO PLAY
OBJECTIVE
Your space station has lost contact with Earth. Survive 12 cycles until rescue arrives. Manage oxygen, power, morale, and hull integrity.
GAMEPLAY
  1. Each cycle presents a crisis with 2-3 choices
  2. Choices affect your four resources — watch the costs
  3. If any resource hits zero, you face a bad ending
  4. Keep your crew alive — losing crew members reduces efficiency
  5. Survive all 12 cycles to be rescued
ENDINGS
There are 5 possible endings depending on your choices, resources, and crew survival. Can you find the best outcome?
