How to Play Imposter

Objective: Find the imposter before they blend in!

Setup: All players except one receive the same secret word. One player is the imposter and doesn't know the word.

Round 1 - Give Clues: Each player gives a one-word clue about their word. The imposter must try to blend in without knowing the word!

Round 2 - Discuss: Players discuss and try to figure out who the imposter is.

Round 3 - Vote: Everyone votes on who they think is the imposter.

Winning: If the imposter is voted out, the players win! If an innocent player is voted out, the imposter wins!
