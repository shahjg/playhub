HOW TO PLAY IMPOSTER

Objective: Find the imposter before it's too late!

Game Setup:

  • One player is randomly selected as the Imposter
  • All other players receive the same secret word
  • The Imposter doesn't know the word

How to Play:

  • Phase 1: Everyone gives a one-word clue about the secret word
  • The Imposter must blend in without knowing the word!
  • Phase 2: Vote on who you think is the Imposter

Winning:

  • Players win if they correctly vote out the Imposter
  • Imposter wins if an innocent player is voted out
