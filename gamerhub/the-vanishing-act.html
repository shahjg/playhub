The Death of Victor Malone

Victor Malone, the world's most celebrated stage illusionist, was found dead in his dressing room at the Meridian Grand Theater thirty minutes before his final performance.
The cause of death: poisoning — a lethal dose of belladonna was found in his pre-show whiskey. The bottle was in his private dressing room, accessible only to those with backstage passes.
Five people had access backstage that evening. One of them wanted Victor's final act to be truly his last. Explore the theater, examine the evidence, and interrogate the suspects.
Pay attention to contradictions. Everyone has a motive. Only one pulled the trigger.
